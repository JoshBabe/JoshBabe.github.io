Factorising Quadratic Trinomials

Factorise -4x^2-20x+24.

Solution

-4x^2-20x+24

Common Factor Factorisation

Common Factor=-4

Factorise the expression by -4 (helps factorise faster and easier).

-4(x^2+5x-6) (Factorise by -4)

Cross Multiplication Method is used.

=-4(x+6)(x-1)

Since the leading term in a bracket must be positive,the minus sign is shifted outside the first bracket making the addition sign in the first sign turn into a minus sign.

Therefore, the solution is -4(x+6)(x-1).
